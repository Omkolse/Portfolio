# DRAIC – Robotics Club Representative Bot

## Overview

DRAIC is a custom-designed robotic mascot created from the **3D interpretation of our robotics club logo**. The robot serves as a **representative interaction platform** for guests, events, and demonstrations, combining expressive design with basic robotic motion and AI-based communication.

The robot is powered by a **Raspberry Pi 3**, enabling it to run a lightweight **chat-based AI system** for conversational interaction. Its expressive design includes animated OLED eyes and simple mechanical movements to create a more engaging and friendly presence during robotics club exhibitions and presentations.

The entire robot structure was **designed in CAD and manufactured using 3D printing**, allowing the logo-inspired character to be transformed into a functional robotic platform.

---

## Key Features

**Logo-Inspired Robot Design**
The robot's form factor is derived directly from the robotics club logo, turning a symbolic design into a physical robotic mascot.

**Interactive AI Communication**
The robot uses a Raspberry Pi 3 to run a **chat-based AI interaction system**, enabling basic communication with visitors and guests.

**Expressive OLED Eyes**
Two small OLED displays are used as **robot eyes**, allowing the robot to display simple emotions and visual expressions.

**Servo-Based Motion System**
Multiple servo motors enable simple yet expressive movements including head rotation, arm motion, and body rotation.

**Fully 3D Printed Structure**
The complete robot body was designed in CAD and optimized for **FDM 3D printing**, making it lightweight, modular, and easy to assemble.

---

## Mechanical Design

The robot structure was designed with a focus on **character aesthetics, structural simplicity, and modular assembly**.

Mechanical capabilities include:

* **Head Rotation** for interactive gestures
* **Arm Movement** (up and down motion) for expressive actions
* **Body Rotation** using a servo-driven base joint
* Internal mounting structures for electronics and servo placement

The modular CAD design allows easy maintenance and future upgrades.

---

## Electronics & Hardware

**Main Controller**

**Actuators**

**Display System**

* 2 × Small OLED displays used as animated eyes

**Power System**
External power supply for servo motors and controller.
